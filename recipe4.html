🟡 Dhokla Recipe

Preparation Time: 40 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Serving: 3 people

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/2 cup curd
  • 1 tsp Eno
  • Salt (as per taste)
  • Mustard seeds
  • Green chilli

👩‍🍳 Steps to Prepare

  1. Mix besan, curd, and water to make a smooth batter.
  2. Add salt and mix well.
  3. Add Eno and pour into a greased tray.
  4. Steam for 15–20 minutes.
  5. Prepare tempering with mustard seeds and chilli.
  6. Pour tempering over dhokla.
  7. Cut into pieces and serve.

💡 Tips

  • Use fresh Eno for soft dhokla.
  • Do not overmix after adding Eno.
