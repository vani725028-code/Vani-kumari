🥞 Dosa Recipe

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Serving: 2 people

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups dosa batter
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Potato masala (optional)

👩‍🍳 Steps to Prepare

  1. Heat a non-stick pan on medium flame.
  2. Pour a ladle of batter and spread in circular motion.
  3. Add a few drops of oil around the edges.
  4. Cook until crispy and golden brown.
  5. Add potato filling if making masala dosa.
  6. Fold and serve hot with chutney.

💡 Tips

  • Use fermented batter for better taste.
  • Cook on medium heat for crispy dosa.
