🍝 Pasta Recipe

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Serving: 2 people

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (puree)
  • Salt (as per taste)
  • Cheese
  • Mixed vegetables

👩‍🍳 Steps to Prepare

  1. Boil water and add pasta. Cook for 8–10 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add chopped onions.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add vegetables and cook properly.
  5. Add boiled pasta and mix well.
  6. Add salt and cheese.
  7. Cook for 2 more minutes and serve hot.

💡 Tips

  • Use fresh vegetables for better taste.
  • Add extra cheese if you like creamy pasta.
