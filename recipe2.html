🍔 Burger Recipe

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty Level: Easy

Serving: 2 people

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 patty
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese slice
  • Sauce (ketchup/mayo)

👩‍🍳 Steps to Prepare

  1. Cook the patty on a pan.
  2. Toast the burger buns.
  3. Apply sauce on buns.
  4. Add lettuce, tomato, and patty.
  5. Add cheese slice.
  6. Close the burger and serve hot.

💡 Tips

  • Use fresh vegetables for better taste.
  • Add extra cheese for a juicy burger.
