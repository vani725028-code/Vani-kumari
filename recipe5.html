🥟 Samosa Recipe

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Difficulty Level: Medium

Serving: 3 people

🛒 Ingredients

  • Maida (flour)
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Peas
  • Spices
  • Oil for frying

👩‍🍳 Steps to Prepare

  1. Prepare dough using maida.
  2. Make potato filling with spices.
  3. Roll dough and shape into cones.
  4. Fill with potato mixture.
  5. Deep fry until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with chutney.

💡 Tips

  • Fry on low flame for crispy samosa.
  • Seal edges properly to avoid opening.
